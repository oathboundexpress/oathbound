Drive With Oathbound Express

Join a professional, organized delivery fleet serving Metro Vancouver. We value our drivers and provide the tools, support, and compensation you deserve.

Why Join Us

Benefits of Driving With Oathbound

We treat our drivers as partners, not just contractors. Here's what sets us apart.

Competitive Compensation

Earn competitive rates with transparent pay structures. Your hard work is recognized and rewarded fairly.

Flexible Scheduling

Choose routes and schedules that work for your lifestyle. We offer both full-time and part-time opportunities.

Full Insurance Coverage

Drive with confidence knowing you're fully covered under our comprehensive WorkSafeBC-compliant insurance program.

Modern Technology

Use our optimized routing app to navigate efficiently. Less time driving, more deliveries completed.

Training & Support

Comprehensive onboarding and ongoing support from our operations team. You're never on your own.

Growth Opportunities

Top performers can advance into team lead and fleet management roles as we continue to grow.

Requirements

What You Need to Get Started

We're looking for reliable, professional individuals who take pride in their work and want to be part of a growing team.

  • Valid BC Driver's License (Class 5 or higher)
  • Clean driving record with no major violations
  • Access to a reliable vehicle (sedan, minivan, or cargo van)
  • Smartphone with GPS capability
  • Ability to lift packages up to 50 lbs
  • Strong knowledge of Metro Vancouver roads and neighborhoods
  • Professional attitude and excellent customer service skills
  • Legal authorization to work in Canada
  • Ability to pass a background check

Ready to Join Our Team?

We're actively recruiting professional drivers across all Metro Vancouver zones. Whether you drive a sedan, minivan, or cargo van — there's a place for you at Oathbound Express.

How to Apply

Send us an email with the following information:

  • 1. Your full name and contact information
  • 2. Type of vehicle you drive
  • 3. Your availability (full-time / part-time)
  • 4. Your preferred delivery zone in Metro Vancouver
  • 5. A copy of your driver's license
